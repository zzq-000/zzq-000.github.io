Troubleshooting Tips

  • App not syncing: Ensure iCloud is enabled and signed in on your device.
  • Missing data: Check if "Local Data Only" mode is turned on under Settings.
  • Import/Export issues: Confirm you have granted Files or iCloud Drive permission.

Data Safety

  • Your data remains local unless you choose to sync with iCloud.
  • No third-party servers or analytics tools are used.
