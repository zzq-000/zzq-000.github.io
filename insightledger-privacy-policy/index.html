InsightLedger Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

InsightLedger ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

2. Data Collection

InsightLedger does not collect or share any personal information with third parties.

All data you create, including receipts, expenses, and notes, stays securely stored on your device.

If you enable iCloud synchronization, your data will be stored privately in your iCloud account, managed by Apple.

3. Local Data and iCloud Storage

InsightLedger uses SwiftData for local data management and iCloud for optional syncing.

We do not have access to, nor do we transmit, your data to external servers.

4. Import and Export

You can freely import or export your data in supported formats (for example, CSV) to maintain full ownership and portability of your financial records.

5. Shortcuts and Automation

If you use Apple Shortcuts with InsightLedger, the automation runs locally on your device.

No shortcut data is sent to our servers.

6. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
